Musk was born on June 28, 1971, in Pretoria, Transvaal, South Africa. The son of Maye Musk (nee Haldeman), a model and dietitian from Regina, Saskatchewan, Canada. Errol Musk, a South African electromechanical engineer, pilot, and sailor. He has a younger brother, Kimbal (born 1972), and a younger sister, Tosca (born 1974). His paternal grandmother was British, and he also has Pennsylvania Dutch ancestry.
